Note: Before you load information for double check make sure purchase orders are synchronised in Web Terminal Synchro page since the purchase order created in QB.

1. Search "Purchase Order" (type first letters (minimum 3) of customer's last name).
2. When Purchase Order found click it to place it to the field and click "List Purchase Order".
3. If purchase order appears correctly click "Generate CSV". ".csv" file will be downloaded and saved to a default downloads folder.
4. Go to US Cabinet Depot website "www.uscabinetdepot.com" and Dealer login.
5. Click "QUICK ORDER" in the top menu on the website.
6. Click "Choose file +" and upload csv file you generated.


7. Review items in the list, then click "Accept" and "Add To Cart"


8. Proceed to "Checkout" and click "+ New Address".
9. Enter customer's shipping address/phone # and click "Ship here".


10. Right click on page and "Save as..." Webpage, Complete (*.htm;*.html).
Name it correctly and save it to appropriate folder.
11. Go back to Terminal window and click "Proceed to Double Check".
12. Choose .html file you just saved and open it for Double Check.
